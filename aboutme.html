About Me

Hello there! 👋

My name is Deniz. I am a UX/UI designer based in Vancouver, BC.

🔍 With seven years of professional experience, I've specialized in data analysis, reporting, and management. Driven by a personal passion for design aesthetics and a genuine interest in crafting user-friendly experiences, I chose to pivot into the design world.

👩🏻‍🏫 I further honed my skills by completing the Web and Mobile App Development Post-Degree Program at Langara College. This immersive experience not only provided me with the technical skills necessary for development but also instilled in me a nuanced understanding of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design principles. Now, armed with this comprehensive education, I am actively seeking opportunities in the UX/UI field.

🌟 I am driven by the prospect of creating solutions that positively impact businesses and am always eager to take on new challenges, pushing the boundaries of innovation.

I'm always open for communication! If you have any questions, thoughts, or ideas, don't hesitate to connect with me on LinkedIn.
