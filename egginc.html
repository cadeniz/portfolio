Egg, Inc.
Game Concept Document

Egg, Inc. is a first-person, 2D isometric mobile simulation game where players establish and expand their chicken farms, conduct research, and advance the game through tapping and clicking actions, ultimately aiming for the end game goal of obtaining the Enlightenment Egg and amassing 57 Vigintillion money. In Egg Inc, players can develop and expand their chicken farms, hire drivers for egg sales, and upgrade their egg-carrying vehicles. By conducting research, players can unlock new advancements, enhancing their farm's productivity and capabilities. They can speed up their progress by using boosts in the game and use the shop section in the game, which allows in-game purchases to help their farms grow. Players can expand their farms and increase their earnings by accepting offers and seizing time-limited offers.
